Favorite All-Time Boxers

Besides playing soccer, I also love boxing as it gives me a great workout. Just as much as I love training, I love watching it and being a fan. Analyzing style matchups and who I believe will win is always fun.

Below are some of my favorite boxers of all-time.

Naoya Inoue (Active)

They call this little guy "The Monster" and it's not hard to tell why. In my opinion, he's one of the top three hardest pound-for-pound punchers in the sport, if not the number one. His iq and speed combined with his power is truly scary!

James Toney (Retired)

This man was as smooth as they come. He hated training with all his heart but loved fighting/sparring. A naturally talented boxer who could've achieved even more if he was truly motivated.

Vasyl Lomachenko (Active)

Angles, punches-in-bunches, and insane footwork is what you get with the two-time gold medalist. The Ukranian is easily one of the most exciting boxers out there to watch currently.

Roy Jones Jr. (Retired)

You have some who are lightning quick and some who have god-given power, but rarely do you see the two combined like with prime Roy Jones Jr.
